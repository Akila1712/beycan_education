FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything Parents Need to Know

What is the best age to start abacus training?

Children can start as early as 5 years old — the perfect age for brain development and number learning.

How long does each level take?

Each level typically lasts 3–6 months, depending on the child’s learning speed and training mode (online or classroom).

How does abacus training help in academics?

Abacus improves calculation speed, memory, focus, and problem-solving, making all academic subjects easier and more enjoyable.

Do I need to buy an abacus tool?

We provide both physical and digital abacus kits, so your child can practice anywhere — no extra purchase needed.
